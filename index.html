Who we are?

Well that's a secret :)

We're a small team of hackers and artists which consists of active Apache CloudStack™ project committers and have deep expertise in developing, deploying and using CloudStack® clouds. We do software development, consultancy, support and training around opensource Apache CloudStack™. Our aim is to help organisations to customize, deploy and manage highly scalable, highly available Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) clouds using Apache CloudStack™ while support ourselves to work full-time on CloudStack®.

Apache CloudStack™ Training

Why do I need training?

Apache CloudStack™ can be set up using the project's online documentation, but in order to get the most out of your installation we recommend taking a training. We have designed our courses to be both personal and applicable. The size of the course-groups is limited to a small number of attendees so there is plenty of time for questions and personal assistance. Training courses are available in different degrees of expertise.
